
# Energy Crisis in Alzheimer’s Mouse Brain


## Motivation
Alzheimer’s disease is characterized by metabolic collapse and selective regional vulnerability. With spatial transcriptomics (10x Xenium), we investigate whether energetic disruption is **localized near amyloid plaques** or reflects a **broader regional phenomenon**.

## Research Questions
1. **Spatial analysis (RQ1):** Does metabolic dysfunction in Alzheimer's follow a spatial gradient around amyloid plaques?

2. **Temporal analysis (RQ2):** Is metabolic decline an early or late event in Alzheimer's progression?

3. **Cell-type analysis (RQ3):** Do different cell types exhibit distinct metabolic responses to Alzheimer's pathology?
